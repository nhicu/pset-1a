Why is Film Important?

Film is more than just a plot or acting. Watching a film is a visual, audio, and sometimes emotional experience. For a few hours, a great film can get you emotionally invested to strangers and unfamiliar places. This emotional connection created between the viewer and a film can impact people in greater ways than first seen. We cry, laugh, and sympathise with these humans and experience a whole different world. One film can take you to a galaxy far far away fighting the evil forces and the next can take you to the harrowing jungles in Vietnam. This does not mean that every film will be good, but great films will always touch a person in a way no other form of art can.

A Bad Film A Decent Film A Great Film

You found it enjoyable to watch for the time being, but would not want to watch again. You are were attentive during the film and ended feeling some type of emotion different from when you started.
